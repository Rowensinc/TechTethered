Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Last updated: 2026

Short version

TechTethered does not sell personal information. We only use information you provide to respond to requests, schedule work, and deliver services.

What we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, phone, business name, city)
  • Project details you send us (issue description, photos you choose to share)

How we use it

  • Reply to your message and provide a quote
  • Schedule service and communicate updates
  • Document your project (only what’s needed to support your setup)

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not share data with advertisers
  • We do not run “data broker” style tracking on this site

Your choices

If you want your message deleted after we finish the job, tell us. If you want to limit what you share, you can — we’ll work with what you’re comfortable providing.

Questions?

Send us a message and we’ll answer straight.
