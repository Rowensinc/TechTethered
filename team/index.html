Get to know our team

TechTethered exists because small businesses deserve reliable networking without being talked down to.

Why TechTethered exists

Most businesses don’t need “enterprise strategy.” They need Wi-Fi that works, ports that aren’t dead, and a closet that isn’t a disaster. We show up on time, do clean work, and explain what we changed.

Our values

  • On time: your schedule matters.
  • Respectful: we treat your space like it’s our own.
  • Clean work: neat runs, labeled cables, organized gear.
  • Clear communication: plain English, no fluff.

Our team

Real people. Real installs. We’ll explain what we’re doing and leave your space cleaner than we found it.

Why background matters

Networking work isn’t just “plug it in.” Every building is different, and a clean result takes someone who’s seen messy wiring, real-world constraints, and the small details that prevent future headaches.

What that means for you

We plan the cleanest path, protect your space, label and test the work, and leave notes you can actually use. You get reliable connections — and you’re not stuck guessing later.
